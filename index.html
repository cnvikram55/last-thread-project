Panipat — The City That Spins Gold
Not a dump. Not a graveyard. Panipat is the Shoddy Yarn Capital of the World — a resourceful recycling engine that imports worn clothing from the UK, USA, and Japan, shreds it, and spins it into low-cost blankets and industrial yarn that keeps millions warm.

08 — Your Move

You now know
where it goes.

🫶
Donate
Before your next purchase, ask: can any of what I own today find a new owner? Donation keeps clothes in circulation for years.
↑ Extends garment life by 2–3 years
♻️
Segregate
Keep old clothes separate from wet waste. A clean, dry garment can be recycled. A contaminated one cannot. This single act changes everything.
↑ Prevents permanent landfilling
🧵
Buy Less
The brands will keep producing. The machine doesn't stop. But the question is whether every new garment you buy is one you truly need.
↓ Reduces post-consumer waste at source
